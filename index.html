About Me

Developer & Innovator

I'm Devang, a passionate developer based in Germany, dedicated to creating innovative solutions that make a difference. Currently, I'm building Super Kalam, a comprehensive platform for mock test examinations that helps students prepare for their academic goals.

With a focus on clean code, user experience, and scalable solutions, I enjoy tackling complex problems and turning ideas into reality. My expertise spans full-stack development, and I'm always eager to learn new technologies and methodologies.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the latest tech trends, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying the beautiful landscapes of Germany.
